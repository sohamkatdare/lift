About us

An exciting and mysterious realm full of wonders and mysteries that we are only now beginning to comprehend lies in a realm full of wonders beyond our imaginations. LIFT is a space travel company that aims to make space travel more accessible and safe. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of human exploration and driving innovation in the field of space exploration. Through the tours and services we provide, we strive for affordable space travel for all.

Starting Up

LIFT was founded with the vision to harness space technology for international development while pursuing space science research and planetary exploration. Our company began with the advancement of space technology through the testing of rockets and space travel. We worked on perfecting a rocket model that would be able to effectively transport people into space.
Dr. Aria Starlight is the founder and chairman of LIFT. He is a renowned scientist and a visionary leader who played a crucial role in establishing the foundation of the commercial space program. Under his guidance, LIFT made significant progress in the fields of satellite communications, meteorology, and remote sensing, among others. Dr. Starlight's contributions to this international space program have had a lasting impact and continue to inspire future generations of space scientists and researchers. His goal was to bring space into our homes and LIFT attempts to capture that same message.
